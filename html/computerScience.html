Autonomous Car

This is a C++ project that I did with my group in my Computer Science 2 class. I came up with an idea of a self-driving car and my group liked the idea. So, we went ahead and did it

What is Autonomus Car?

An autonomous car, also known as a self-driving car or driverless car, is a vehicle that is capable of navigating and operating without human intervention. It uses a combination of advanced technologies, including sensors, cameras, radar, lidar, GPS, and artificial intelligence (AI), to perceive its surroundings, make decisions, and control its movements. We took the sensor part of it and implemented that in our mini autonomus car. That gave it the ability to guid it self and avoid obstacles.
